Unit Conversion Information

Length

  • 10 millimeters (mm) = 1 centimeter (cm)
  • 1000 millimeters (mm) = 1 meter (m)
  • 1000000 millimeters (mm) = 1 kilometer (km)
  • 25.4 millimeters (mm) = 1 inch (in)
  • 1609344 millimeters (mm) = 1 mile (mi)

Mass

  • 1000 gram(g) = 1 kilogram(kg)
  • 453.592 gram(g) = 1 Pound(lb)
  • 28.3495 gram(g) = 1 Ounce(oz)

Temperature

  • 100° Celsius(C) = 212° Fahrenheit(F)
  • 100° Celsius(C) = 373.15 Kelvin(K)

Volume

  • 1000 mililiter(mL) = 1 Litre(L)
  • 3785.41 mililiter(mL) = 1 Gallon(Gal)
  • 29.5735 mililiter(mL) = 1 Fluid ounce(floz)
